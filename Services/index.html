WHAT WE PROVIDE?

Intersection Counts

    We specialize in processing intersection counts with extensive data across global locations. Our services include comprehensive classification of vehicle movements. The Intersection Count provides precise data on straight, left-turn, right-turn, and U-turn movements at intersections. We handle complex junctions for durations such as 12 hours or 48 hours. Additionally, our intersection count services captures a wide range of user-specified information to meet diverse analytical needs.

Roundabout Counts

    We specialize in processing roundabout traffic counts using extensive data from global locations. Our expertise includes detailed classification of vehicle movements within roundabouts. We accurately analyze the Total Turning Movement (TMV), capturing details of entry, exit, and circulation within each leg of the roundabout. Our team is skilled in handling roundabouts with varying complexities, including 4-arm and 5-arm configurations, using advanced video analysis techniques. 

Pedestrian Counts

      We provide comprehensive Pedestrian Count services for various locations, including sidewalks, street corners, intersections, parks, and retail store entrances. Our team accurately analyzes pedestrian movements alongside vehicle classifications at any junction. This data is invaluable for transport departments in planning infrastructure such as footbridges near busy junctions, helping to minimize traffic disruptions and ensure smoother flow for both vehicles and pedestrians.
