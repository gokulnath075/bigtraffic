What we do?

          We specialize in processing intersection counts involving large data volumes from global locations. Our services include detailed classification of vehicle movements, pedestrian activity, and bicycles passing through intersections. Using Turning Movement Counts (TMC), we provide precise data on straight, left-turn, right-turn, and U-turn movements for each leg of the intersection. We process complex junctions for durations of 12, 24 and 48 hours, capturing delay data, cycle movements, pedestrian counts, and other user-specific metrics.

"Our motto"
